KitchenCRATE Helps Bring Your Dream Kitchen to Life

Kitchen remodeling can be a stressful undertaking, but kitchenCRATE was created to turn kitchen remodeling into a fast, fun process with incredible results. Our kitchenCRATE system delivers the kitchen of your dreams on a strict timeline and budget that you can count on, guaranteed!

We offer three levels of kitchen remodeling — kitchenCRATE Refinish, kitchenCRATE Classic, and kitchenCRATE Custom remodel. Depending on which one you choose, the experience can vary a bit, but here’s what you can expect when you work with us:

  • Free consultation — We’ll meet you in your home to discuss the details of your project, take measurements, draw diagrams, and answer your questions.
  • Proposal review — You will meet your sales professional to discuss each element of your project and the corresponding costs, all clearly laid out in the proposal.
  • Choose your style and materials — Choose your preferred countertop and other materials, and for a Classic or Custom kitchen remodel, you’ll have a three to four-hour session with one of our certified interior designers to plan out the entire project.
  • Launch meeting — Your project manager will review your EXACT project schedule, confirm your selections, and answer any final questions you have.
  • CRATE in action! — One CRATE containing all the materials for your remodel will be delivered to your house the day before our crews arrive to do the work. KitchenCRATE projects are typically completed in as little as 9 days, depending on project scope.

When we’re done with your new kitchen, it’s your turn to enjoy your space for decades to come!

KitchenCRATE Makes Your Kitchen Remodel Fun, Fast & Flawless

Say goodbye to the stress and confusion of kitchen remodeling! KitchenCRATE was founded in 2012 on the belief that kitchen remodeling projects should be an enjoyable experience for homeowners. Scott Monday came up with the idea while shopping at a members-only discount store with his wife. As they walked down the aisles, they joked about how they wished a helicopter could deliver crates full of all the materials they needed for a kitchen remodel, and that the renovation could be finished in less than a week. And then Scott thought, why not? Why couldn’t kitchen remodels be streamlined into a system that’s repeated over and over? Even though the materials might change, kitchen remodeling jobs are similar enough that they can follow a formula that enhances efficiency, reduces cost, and improves the overall experience and results. That’s why homeowners are choosing kitchenCRATE for their kitchen renovations. From the initial meeting to the project’s completion, homeowners enjoy the best of all worlds — great communication, transparent pricing, dependable and fast timelines, and a stunning finished project. To us, there’s nothing better than the look on a customer’s face when they see the completed kitchen of their dreams. To learn more, contact kitchenCRATE today.
