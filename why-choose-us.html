KitchenCRATE – Where Excellence Isn’t Just a Goal, It’s a Given

Here at kitchenCRATE, our process is what really sets us apart from other kitchen remodelers. In nine easy steps, we can transform your kitchen from boring to breathtaking:

  1. The phone consultation — Chat with us over the phone so we can learn more about your project.
  2. The free in-home consultation — We’ll meet with you for one-two hours to discuss the details of your kitchen remodel, take measurements, draw diagrams, and answer your questions.
  3. The proposal review meeting — Meet with your project manager to discuss each element of your remodel and its cost.
  4. Countertop selection — Take a trip to a stone showroom and choose your exact countertop material.
  5. Interior design meeting — For a Classic or Custom kitchen remodel, a free two-hour session with a certified interior designer will help you make the perfect selections for your project.
  6. aunch meeting — Have one final meeting with your project superintendent to review your schedule, confirm your selections, and ask any final questions about your project.
  7. CRATE delivery — The CRATE will be delivered to your home on the day before our crews arrive on time to start your project!
  8. Wrap up — When we’re done, our team will review the project with you and note any final items needing attention.
  9. Enjoy! — Now it’s time to enjoy your new kitchen for decades to come!
